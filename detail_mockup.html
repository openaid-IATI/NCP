H2O Health Plus (H2O+)

Water, sanitation, and fuel-efficient stoves for cost of water alone

The project will mitigate the dire health/economic situations of Noterhern Ugandan villagers, especially women/girls, by addressing 3 conditions most responsible for their plight: lack of clean water, poor hygiene/sanitation, and open fire cooking.
